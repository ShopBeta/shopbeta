ShopBeta - About us

ShopBeta Online Mall is a multi – vendor social commerce platform in designed for the small and medium scale businesses. The Online Mall is tailored for the average shop owner to own a free online store that can automate their business processes. Retail shops can now own a free online shop in less than one minute. ShopBeta makes it possible for online store owners to organize products online, customize your store, track and respond to orders, collect payments and have goods delivered at the doorstep of clients. Our range of services are designed to ensure optimum levels of convenience and customer satisfaction with the retail process; these services include our lowest price guarantee, 7-day free return policy*, order delivery-tracking, dedicated customer service support and many other premium services. As we continue to expand the platform, our scope of offerings will increase in variety, simplicity and convenience; join us and enjoy the increasing benefits. It’s easy to find the right products at a competitive price from our unexhaustive categories for every moment – on your phone, your computer, your tablet and more.

Vision

The vision is to build the most reliable marketplace in the world across various different platforms or media like the metaverse. ShopBeta is committed to service the digital world with seemless experiences and services to provide smoother market transactions using social commerce.

Mission

Our mission is to digitalize market economies all over the world however necessary even if it entails entering various markets to expand our portfolio of investments and influence and also making live easier for consumers through a lower price click experience. The essence of ShopBeta’s identity and purpose of existence is guided by Simplicity, innovation, Quality, honesty and integrity as our core values. Thank you and we hope you enjoy your experience with us!
